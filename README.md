# MARS COLONY

#### Game summary
In year 2100, Earth starts to be very polluted and running out of resources. People realize, that Earth is unfit for living so they start to think of a solution to that problem. The solution seems to be to colonize Mars. Mars Colony is a turn based strategy game, where the main objective is to colonize and teraform Mars so people can live there. The two main issues in colonizing are, that there is not enough water and the atmosphere isn't dense enough. There are also several huge corporates back on Earth, that have their own agendas on Mars. You can win their favor and thus their support by helping them fulfill their agendas. As the time goes on, poeple on Earth start to realize importance of your cause and humanitarian packages from corporates and from other people start to be more significant and also people on Earth will research new technologies, which can affect your gameplay as well. But after a certain period, sitation on Earth starts to be so unstable that people will send less and less help, due to lack of transportships, their own needs and after they will start to die out and you will be left without any help. Your colony will also be affected by natural disasters such as sandstorms, floods (once you start to actually accumulate some water), volcano eruptions and some catastrophes that manking has never seen before. Your goal is to have self sustainable colony even when you stop recieving support from Earth.

#### Core mechanics
resource management - you have to feed your people, have resources to build crutial buildings...
politics - you need to win favor of corporates that can help you
managing cities (oxygen and water) - your cities need oxygen for people either from atmosphere or you can somehow isolate city in greenhouse or another technology 
people's work management - you have to assign jobs to your people so you yields are as effective as possible

#### Gameplay
turn 1
You start a game and are presented a hexmap full of empty sand places. You are also granted one cargo ship, greenhouse, excavator, personel counting 10 people and food for 20 turns to feed 10 of your people. You place your greenhouse somewhere and assign 5 workers there (the more workers you assign, the more productive it will be). You send excavator with remaining people to discover what is under the sand right next to your greenhouse and end your turn.
turn 3
Your excavator finds a weird mineral and you assign one person, to bring it back to Earth for further research. Then you assign excavator to another place.
turn 5 
Your cargoship gets home and you immediately get an offer from one of the major corporates that they will provide specialized drills and personel if you keep brinigng the some amount of this mineral. You accept and they put drill and 5 miners on a ship and send it to you.
turn 6
Excavator finds feritile soil that will speed up your agriculture in future.

#### Music
In the early game, you listen to calm and rather simple orchestra songs so you as a player feel curious and ready to explore new planet. The more the game progresses, the soundtrack starts to be more industrial and epic. You hear epic civilization style orchestra. In the endgame you hear more catastrohpic and dramatic music, that implicates that the stakes are high, mankinds destiny lies on your shoulders and there is little margin for error.

#### Art Style
The whole game you see only your game map from above. Every object in game has its own pictogram. These pictograms are hand drawed and might have some slight animations. The game is bright from the beginning but the general tone changes according to how well you are doing. If you lose a lot of people over a period of several turns, the general tone gets darker and more depressing.
